# Chinese Sweet and Sour SauceÂ 

## Ingredients

* 1/2 cup ketchup
* 1 cup white sugar
* 1/4 cup vinegar
* 3 tbsp cornstarch
* 3 cups water
* 1-2 tbsp soy sauce

## Directions

* Mix cornstarch in with sugar. 
* Add cold water. 
* Add all other ingredients. 
* Bring to a boil. 
* Add sauce to cooked meatballs. 
* Heat up in oven.
