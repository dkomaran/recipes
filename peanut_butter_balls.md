# Peanut Butter Balls

- ½ cup peanut butter
- ¼ cup honey
- 1 cup rolled oats
- ½ cup chopped peanuts
- ½ cup chocolate chips

-or-

Omit peanuts and chocolate chips and replace with 1 cup reese’s pieces candy. Add more peanut butter, honey, rolled oats if using extra candy.

Rolls into balls

Put in fridge.
